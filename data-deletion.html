Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request deletion of their personal data from ASOS (AI Sales Operating System).

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to privacy@getaisales.com with subject: Data Deletion Request.

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Your WhatsApp number used with ASOS
  • Your business/account name (if applicable)
  • Any additional details that help identify your record

What We Delete

  • Contact profile information linked to your request
  • Conversation/message history stored by ASOS
  • Related lead and engagement records associated with your identifier

Processing Time

We process verified deletion requests within 30 days of receipt. In many cases, deletion is completed sooner.

Identity Verification

To protect your privacy, we may ask for additional information to verify that the request is made by the correct data subject or authorized representative.

If legal, security, billing, or fraud-prevention obligations apply, we may retain limited records as required by law.
