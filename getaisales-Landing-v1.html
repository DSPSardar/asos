How It Works

From ad click to
closed deal — automated.

1

Customer clicks your Meta Ad

Your Click-to-WhatsApp ad fires. The customer lands in your WhatsApp Business chat. Campaign attribution (ad ID, adset, campaign) is captured automatically.

⬗ Meta Ads
2

Claude AI takes over instantly

Within 30 seconds, Claude replies with a personalised message in your brand voice. It asks qualification questions naturally — budget, need, timeline, authority.

◎ Claude AI
3

Lead is scored and CRM updated

Every response updates the lead's score (HOT / WARM / COLD), stage in the pipeline, and BANT qualification data — automatically, in real time.

◈ CRM
4

HOT leads handed to your agent

When the AI detects buying intent, it alerts your sales agent instantly and transfers the conversation with a full context brief — no cold handoffs.

🔥 Handoff
5

Deal closed. Meta CAPI fires.

On every close, a server-side Purchase event fires to Meta Conversions API — feeding the algorithm real revenue data to optimise your next campaign.
