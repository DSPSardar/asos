How It Works

From ad click to
closed deal — automated.

Five steps. Zero manual work. The AI runs the whole pipeline; your team only steps in for the closes that matter.

1

Customer clicks your Meta Ad

Click-to-WhatsApp ad fires. The customer lands in your WhatsApp Business chat. Campaign attribution (ad ID, adset, campaign) is captured automatically.

META ADS
2

Claude AI takes over instantly

Within 30 seconds, the Qualifier agent replies in your brand voice. It asks BANT questions naturally — budget, need, timeline, authority — and reads sentiment.

CLAUDE AI
3

Lead is scored and CRM updated

Every response updates lead score (HOT / WARM / COLD), stage, and BANT data — in real time. Pipeline is always current; no manual entry.

When the AI detects buying intent, the Closer agent alerts your sales rep instantly with a full context brief. No cold handoffs, no lost deals.

HANDOFF

On every close, a server-side Purchase event fires to Meta Conversions API — feeding the algorithm real revenue data to optimise your next campaign.
