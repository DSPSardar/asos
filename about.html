About us

We build the AI sales engine for WhatsApp.

getaisales is the operating system every modern sales team plugs into Meta Ads and WhatsApp — so qualifying, replying and closing happens 24/7, automatically.

Our story

We started getaisales in 2024 after watching brilliant founders burn weekends replying to WhatsApp leads at midnight. Meta Ads were generating clicks. The clicks were generating chats. The chats were dying because nobody could keep up.

So we built the system we wished existed: an AI sales agent that runs the entire pipeline — from the moment someone clicks an ad to the moment they're ready to buy — without missing a message, mistaking a tire-kicker for a hot lead, or going to sleep at 2 AM.

What we believe

  • Sales should never sleep. Your buyers are awake at 11 PM on a Saturday. Your pipeline should be too.
  • Conversations are data. Every WhatsApp thread is a goldmine of intent — if you have software smart enough to mine it.
  • Humans close. AI qualifies. The best sales teams use AI to triage and prep, then jump in to close the deals that matter.
  • Attribution beats vibes. If you can't tell which ad closed the deal, you're guessing. We end the guessing.
