Cookie Policy.

How and why we use cookies and similar technologies on getaisales.com.

Last updated: April 24, 2026 · Effective: April 24, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember you, keep you logged in, understand how the Service is used, and (with consent) measure marketing campaigns.

2. Categories of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the site to function — authentication, session, CSRF protection, load balancing. Cannot be disabled.

  • session_id — keeps you logged in (24h)
  • csrf_token — prevents cross-site request forgery (session)
  • tenant_id — routes you to your workspace (24h)

Functional

Remember your preferences (theme, language, last-viewed dashboard).

  • ui_prefs — sidebar collapsed state, theme (12 months)
  • last_workspace — your most recent active workspace (12 months)

Analytics

Help us understand how the Service is used so we can improve it. Set only with your consent.

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics 4 (13 months)
  • posthog — product analytics (12 months)

Marketing

Help us measure the performance of our ads. Set only with your consent.

  • _fbp — Meta Pixel (90 days)
  • li_sugr — LinkedIn Insight Tag (90 days)

3. Managing your preferences

You can change your cookie choices at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in our footer or via your browser controls. Disabling strictly necessary cookies will break parts of the Service.

4. Do Not Track

Because there is no industry standard for DNT signals, we do not currently respond to them. We honor explicit opt-outs through the cookie banner.

5. Changes

We will update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
